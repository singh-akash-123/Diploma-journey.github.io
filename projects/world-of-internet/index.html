How Internet Works

There are many people who don't know how internet works or may be they think that Internet are dependent on satellite and totally run due to satellite.

But, It's not true, Internet are not fully dependent on satellite.In fact, 99% internet are works by Fiber optic cable or also known as Submarine cables.

The information you shared on the internet or you need to access from the internet is tranferred by this fibre optic cable.It is laid under the sea level to carry out telecommunication signals from servers to different submarine-cables points.The first submarine communications cables laid begginning in the 1850s carried telegraphy traffic, establishing the first instant telecommunications links between continents, such as the first transatlantic telegraph cable which bacame operational on 16 August 1858.

If you want to access any file or data then your request are transferred by the tier 2 or tier 3 company to the submarine cable points.After reaching to the submarine points, it select any of the available root for reach to that particular server and this is handled by the particular software.
